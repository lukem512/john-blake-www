Richard Blake, Touch of Wood

I am a woodturner based in Pembrokeshire. I turn bespoke pieces and make art from local wood.

My services include one-to-one and group woodturning tuition, custom order, reproduction and repair work. I also sell various pieces in local craft shops.

Registered Professional Turner as recognised by Worshipful Company of Turners London.
