Richard Blake, Touch of Wood

My name is Richard J Blake and like most lads back in the early 60â€™s in schools before health and safety, I was given the opportunity to use a woodturning lathe for the first time with predictable results, a piece of fast revolving wood ejecting itself from the lathe in an arc across the workshop!

I am pleased to report after almost 60 years my skill at the lathe has improved to the extent I am now a Registered Professional Turner recognised by Worshipful Company of Turners London.

My workshop is in Pembroke, West Wales, where I turn mostly locally grown wood, both functional and artistic design, take on commissions and tutor novice to experienced turners in the various skills needed to safely produce a wood turned item of varying complexity.

Products and Tuition

My products include bowls, tealight holders, spinning tops and pens. You can find examples of my work in the Gallery.

I offer individual and group tuition and I have experience teaching all abilities, from complete beginner to experienced.
