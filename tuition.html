Teaching and Tuition

Richard is an approved Woodturning Tutor as recognised by the Association of Woodturners of Great Britain. He is also a registered Professional Turner supported by the Worshipful Company of Turners London.

Richard usually teaches woodturning techniques at his home workshop in Pembroke.

Pricing

These courses are an example of the tuition on offer, although Richard will structure his tuition to fit the students' experience and individual needs.

Course Covers Sessions Price
Taster Workshop and lathe safety, introduction to tools and their use Single 3 hour session learning safe use of the lathe and tools. You will make a small turned item to take home. £60
Beginner Faceplate and spindle turning, tool sharpening and finishing A 6 hour session in which you will get to grips with using the lathe and a variety of tools: roughing spindle gouge, spindle gouge, bowl gouge and skew chisel. Make a tea light, medium-sized bowl, light pull or pen. £120
Improver Learn the finer points of faceplate and bowl turning and tool sharpening A 6 hour session in which you'll learn superior cuts on faceplate and spindle work. You will make a piece of your choice to take home. £130
Experienced Texturing, colouring and other techniques to widen your woodturning skills A 6 hour session to explore different techniques that will maek your turnings stand out. Sessions will be catered to your choice of subject. £140

A 10% discount is available for booking 3 or more sessions in advance.

All safety equipment and PPE will be provided for each session. Wood, abrasives and finishes will also provided. For more experienced or advanced sessions, there may be an extra charge for large or exotic wood stock. You can, of course, bring your own.

Beginners and improver students can bring their own tools to be sharpened to their individual requirements.

During 6 hour sessions, we will aim to start at 10am. We will break for lunch at 1pm, when a light lunch such as a sandwich will be provided. The afternoon session will then continue from 3pm until 5pm. Coffee and tea as required.

Richard looks forward to meeting you and helping to advance your woodturning experience.
